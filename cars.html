From our inception, Team GigaWatt has continually pushed the boundaries of electric vehicle design and performance. Each generation of our cars represents significant innovations in technology, performance, and sustainability. Scroll down to explore the evolution of our Formula Student vehicles.

GIGAWATT 3.0

FORMULA STUDENT 2023

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Top Speed: 120 km/h
  • Acceleration (0-100km/h): 3.8 seconds
  • Motor: Dual EMRAX 228 Permanent Magnet Synchronous
  • Maximum Power: 80 kW
  • Maximum Torque: 240 Nm
  • Battery: 400V Lithium-Ion, 7.5 kWh capacity
  • Weight: 230 kg
  • Chassis: Carbon Fiber Monocoque
  • Suspension: Double Wishbone, Pushrod Actuated
  • Brakes: 4-Piston Calipers, Ventilated Rotors
  • Tires: Hoosier 20.5x7.0-13 R25B
  • Aerodynamics: Front & Rear Wings, Full Underbody

KEY INNOVATIONS

Lightweight Design

Carbon fiber monocoque chassis reduces weight by 15% compared to previous models while increasing structural integrity.

Advanced Battery Management

Custom BMS with active cell balancing and thermal management improves efficiency and extends battery life.

Aerodynamic Package

CFD-optimized aero package generates 120kg of downforce at 80km/h, enhancing cornering capabilities.

Intelligent Traction Control

Custom-developed traction control system with multiple driving modes optimizes performance across varying conditions.

DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY

GigaWatt 3.0 represents the culmination of our team's experience and innovations. Development began in June 2022, with a focus on improving power-to-weight ratio and overall efficiency. Our interdisciplinary team spent over 8,000 hours designing, manufacturing, and testing this vehicle.

Through extensive simulation and prototype testing, we achieved a 20% increase in overall performance compared to our previous model. Key improvements include a redesigned powertrain, enhanced energy recovery system, and advanced composite materials for structural components.

The car underwent rigorous testing at the BITS Pilani Goa Campus proving grounds, covering more than 500 km in various test scenarios before competition readiness.

GIGAWATT 2.0

FORMULA BHARAT 2022

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Top Speed: 110 km/h
  • Acceleration (0-100km/h): 4.2 seconds
  • Motor: Single EMRAX 228 Permanent Magnet Synchronous
  • Maximum Power: 65 kW
  • Maximum Torque: 200 Nm
  • Battery: 380V Lithium-Ion, 6.5 kWh capacity
  • Weight: 245 kg
  • Chassis: Hybrid Steel-Aluminum Space Frame
  • Suspension: Double Wishbone, Pushrod Actuated
  • Brakes: 4-Piston Calipers, Solid Rotors
  • Tires: Hoosier 20.5x7.0-13 R25B
  • Aerodynamics: Front & Rear Wings

KEY INNOVATIONS

Enhanced Battery Design

Redesigned battery pack architecture increased energy density by 18% while improving safety features.

Custom Motor Controller

In-house developed motor controller improved torque delivery and responsiveness throughout the power band.

Optimized Weight Distribution

45:55 front-to-rear weight distribution enhanced handling characteristics and cornering stability.

Telemetry System

Real-time data monitoring and analysis system for performance optimization during testing and racing.

DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY

GigaWatt 2.0 marked a significant step forward in our design philosophy. Development began in May 2021, with a strong emphasis on electrical system reliability and chassis optimization. The team dedicated over 6,500 hours to bringing this design to life.

This iteration featured our first custom Battery Management System (BMS), which allowed for more precise control over battery performance and safety. The chassis design evolved from our first prototype, incorporating lessons learned to enhance driver ergonomics and accessibility.

The car was extensively tested at regional tracks and on campus, undergoing numerous design iterations based on empirical data collected during test sessions.

GIGAWATT 1.0

SUPRA 2021

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Top Speed: 100 km/h
  • Acceleration (0-100km/h): 4.8 seconds
  • Motor: Emrax 207 Permanent Magnet Synchronous
  • Maximum Power: 50 kW
  • Maximum Torque: 160 Nm
  • Battery: 350V Lithium-Ion, 5.8 kWh capacity
  • Weight: 260 kg
  • Chassis: Steel Tubular Space Frame
  • Suspension: Double Wishbone, Direct Actuated
  • Brakes: 2-Piston Calipers, Solid Rotors
  • Tires: Hoosier 20.0x7.0-13 R25B
  • Aerodynamics: Basic Front Wing

KEY INNOVATIONS

Safety Systems

Comprehensive electrical safety features including insulation monitoring and emergency shutdown systems.

Modular Design

Easily accessible components and modular assembly for efficient maintenance and troubleshooting.

Cooling System

Liquid cooling system for the motor and controllers to ensure optimal performance during extended operation.

Integrated Systems

CAN bus communication network connecting all electronic control units for centralized management.

DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY

GigaWatt 1.0 was our foundation-the first electric race car built by Team GigaWatt. Development began in July 2020, focused on creating a reliable platform that would establish our presence in Formula Student competitions.

As our inaugural vehicle, the development process involved extensive research, learning, and overcoming numerous challenges. The team worked closely with faculty advisors and industry partners to navigate the complexities of electric vehicle design and safety requirements.

Despite being our first attempt, GigaWatt 1.0 successfully passed all technical inspections at SUPRA 2021, demonstrating the team's commitment to engineering excellence and attention to safety standards.
