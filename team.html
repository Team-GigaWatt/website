THE DRIVING FORCE

Team GigaWatt comprises over 40 dedicated undergraduate students from various disciplines at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus. United by their passion for automotive engineering and sustainable technology, these talented individuals collaborate across specialized subsystems to design, build, and race electric formula-style vehicles.

Our team members develop practical skills in engineering, project management, fundraising, and teamwork that extend far beyond the classroom. The challenging environment of Formula Student competition fosters innovation, perseverance, and leadership-qualities that prepare our members for successful careers in engineering and beyond.
