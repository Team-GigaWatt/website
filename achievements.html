TECHNICAL INNOVATIONS

Advanced Battery Management System

Our in-house developed BMS with active cell balancing and multi-stage fault protection earned the "Most Innovative Electric System" award at Formula Bharat 2023. The system achieved 98.5% energy efficiency while maintaining optimal thermal performance.

Torque Vectoring System

The dual-motor torque vectoring system developed for GigaWatt 3.0 provides precise power distribution between wheels, reducing lap times by 8%. This technology was recognized with the "Technical Innovation Award" at Formula Student UK 2023.

Lightweight Composite Chassis

Our carbon fiber monocoque chassis design reduced overall vehicle weight by 15% while increasing torsional stiffness by 32% compared to our previous tubular space frame design, significantly improving vehicle dynamics and handling.

Aerodynamic Package

The CFD-optimized aerodynamic package on GigaWatt 3.0 generates 120kg of downforce at 80km/h with minimal drag increase, enhancing cornering speeds and overall lap performance. The design received acclaim at multiple competition design events.

Custom Telemetry System

Our proprietary telemetry system captures and analyzes 50+ parameters in real-time, allowing for data-driven optimization of vehicle setup and race strategy. This system was highlighted as an exemplary engineering solution at Formula Bharat 2023.

Digital Twin Simulation

Development of a comprehensive vehicle simulation platform that accurately predicts real-world performance within 5% margin, significantly reducing development time and costs while optimizing design decisions.
